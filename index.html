    About Berry Bliss Bakery

    Welcome to Berry Bliss Bakery, where every cake is crafted with passion, creativity, and the finest ingredients.

    Founded in 2018 in Baghdad, Iraq, Berry Bliss Bakery began as a small family-owned business with a dream of bringing joy to every celebration through handmade desserts.

    Over the years, we have earned the trust of hundreds of customers by delivering beautifully designed cakes, cupcakes, and sweet treats for birthdays, weddings, graduations, and special occasions.

    Our team of talented bakers carefully prepares each order using fresh ingredients, premium creams, and unique recipes that combine taste and elegance.
